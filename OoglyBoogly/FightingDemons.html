Fighting Demons is the second post-humous album released by Grade A Productions and Interscope Records released on December 10th, 2021.

It featured 21 songs 3 of which were interludes with Juice WRLD speaking from past interviews and Eminem speaking on Juice WRLD the rest are songs with 3 having features from Polo G, Trippie Redd, Suga of BTS, and Justin Bieber.

This one included songs that explained a feeling or sense of new places, how he or others might feel about current situations, a sense of something bad thats gonna happen, and different personalities.

This album was Juice WRLDs 4th No. 1 on Billboards Top R&B/Hip-Hop albums chart.
