This album was his 3rd album released July 10th 2020.

It was released posthumously by Grade A productions and Interscope Records following the tragic death of Juice WRLD in 2019.

This album had 21 tracks featuring 4 interludes and 17 actual songs with features from The Weeknd, Trippie Redd, Halsey, The Kid Laroi, and others.

It had a Hip Hop and RnB type of feel with an upbeat sound but a deeper meaning in the songs.
